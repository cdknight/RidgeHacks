What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where students individually or collaboratively create innovative solutions with technology, often using hardware, software, or both! Students present and share their ideas with other participants and hope to be rewarded with prizes for their work. Hackathons are great learning opportunities for everyone, no matter their experience level. Overall, hackathons are an excellent event for any student with an interest in computer science and programming to attend.
